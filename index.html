Store Update

Since 2020, License To Gay has proudly created and shipped our aluminium wallet cards to customers in over 50 countries, with more than 10,000 items sold. What started as a small idea grew into something truly special, and I’m incredibly grateful for all the support I've received along the way.

As a one-person business alongside being a single parent and managing many other commitments, I’ve reached a point where I can no longer give this shop the time and energy it deserves. A huge percentage of our orders are from the United States and unfortunately postage costs have risen approximately 6x on top of the ongoing tariff and import tax challenges which have made it increasingly difficult to continue as things used to be. For now, I have made the difficult decision to close this store.

It's never easy to say goodbye, but thank you all for being part of this journey. 💖

If you are a previous customer and need any assistance with a past order, please feel free to get in touch at licensetogay@gmail.com.
